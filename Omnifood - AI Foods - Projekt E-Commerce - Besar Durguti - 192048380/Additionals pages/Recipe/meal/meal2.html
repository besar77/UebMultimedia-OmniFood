Beef and Broccoli

Super quick prep, kid-friendly

Prep time: 8 min

Oven time: 15 min

Yield: 4 servings

Cost: $6 per serving / $25 total

Our no-fry beef and broccoli gives you a tasty classic with less sugar and salt.

Combining Everything Bagel seasoning with soy and teriyaki sauces introduces added umami flavor, without any of the complicated prep. Balanced with a side of brown rice, this hearty meal will be done and on your table before takeout is even ready.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce - 1 teaspoon
  • Cooked rice - 20 oz
  • Stir-fry beef - 16 oz
  • Broccoli florets - 12 oz
  • Teriyaki sauce - 0.5 cup
  • Everything bagel spice - 2 teaspoon
